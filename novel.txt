He didn't know why they were there... but they were.
The crowd stiood stock still, silent, moonlight falling through the trees like notched blades broken from unspeakable horrors. 

They were confused, but they were learning. With help from some great TAs, the group managed to push and pull their first assignment.

They had some minor difficulties, but managed to prevail. 

The assignment was malevolent from inception, demanding of the crowd a multitude of skill absent until that day. The crowd lurched forward in the darkness, gutteral yawps from mouths of shattered teeth, throats coated in old blood, ruined shoes from endless shamboling, and a thirst for brain...



Repetition was the key to this game, and the team know it would enhance their comprehension.

The crowd believed the more brains they consumed the greater their comprehension would become. The crowd began to turn on each other, until there was one last survivor.


Brain consumption was at an all time high, and the crowd was clammoring for the last piece. Alas, there was one survivor, who had gained the intelligence of the others.

The intelligence festered in his mind, contorting his body and face into a gnarled fiend of the shadows until, as if this horrific reality was the only outcome for him, the Slenderman was born...AND THE SLENDERMAN WAS ME:
